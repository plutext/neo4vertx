Bridging the gap between Vert.x and Neo4j

Maven

You can add the neo4vertx-core dependency to you project to include neo4vertx.

<dependency>
    <artifactId>neo4vertx-core</artifactId>
    <groupId>org.openpcf</groupId>
    <version>2.0.0-SNAPSHOT</version>
</dependency>
